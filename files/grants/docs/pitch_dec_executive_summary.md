## **Pitch Deck Executive Summary (One Paragraph)**

AdaRSS is a Career Stability Intelligence Layer that helps individuals, institutions, and governments understand which skills create durable, long-term value in a rapidly evolving economy. Today’s workforce systems are misaligned with technological change—people invest years learning skills that quickly become obsolete, while education providers and employers lack reliable intelligence to distinguish foundational competencies from short-lived trends. AdaRSS solves this by applying machine learning to classify skills into enduring, emergent, and transient categories based on their long-term economic stability and demand trajectories. This enables data-driven decisions in education design, workforce planning, and talent investment, reducing skill waste and improving employability outcomes at scale. Positioned as infrastructure rather than a standalone application, AdaRSS integrates into existing labor, education, and policy systems to power predictive workforce intelligence for a more resilient global economy.
