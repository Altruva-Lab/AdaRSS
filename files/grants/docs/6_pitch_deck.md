# **AdaRSS Pitch Deck**

---

## **Slide 1: Title**

**AdaRSS**
*Career Stability Intelligence for a Rapidly Changing Economy*

Building the world’s workforce intelligence layer for skill durability, career resilience, and future-of-work decision-making.

---

## **Slide 2: The Problem**

The global workforce is structurally misaligned with technological change:

* Skills evolve faster than education systems can adapt
* Workers invest years in skills that quickly become obsolete
* Employers lack visibility into long-term skill value
* Governments and institutions cannot reliably forecast workforce needs
* Emerging economies are disproportionately affected

**Result:** workforce instability, wasted education investment, and reduced economic mobility.

---

## **Slide 3: Why This Is Happening**

Current systems optimize for *short-term demand*, not *long-term skill durability*:

* Job markets signal “what is popular now,” not “what lasts”
* Curricula are reactive, not predictive
* Training ecosystems lack intelligence on skill half-life
* No system exists to measure skill stability over time

There is no “stability layer” for the global workforce.

---

## **Slide 4: The Insight**

Skills are not equal in economic time value.

Every skill can be classified by its **long-term durability across technological cycles**:

* Some remain foundational
* Some grow rapidly
* Some decay quickly

This can be modeled, learned, and predicted.

---

## **Slide 5: The Solution**

**AdaRSS introduces a Career Stability Intelligence Layer**

We classify all skills into:

* **0 — Enduring Foundations**
  Long-term, cross-industry durable skills

* **1 — Emergent Necessities**
  High-growth, rising demand skills

* **2 — Transient Skills**
  Short-lived, technology-dependent skills

This transforms unstructured labor data into actionable intelligence.
