## 🧠 SOLUTION

AdaRSS is a workforce intelligence system that classifies skills based on their long-term durability in the economy.

We organize every skill into three categories:

Enduring Foundations…

Emergent Necessities…

and Transient Skills.

Enduring skills are the ones that survive technological shifts.

Emergent skills are rising with market demand.

And transient skills are tied to short-lived tools or trends.

This shifts decision-making from guesswork…

to structured intelligence.
