## **About AdaRSS**

AdaRSS is building the intelligence layer for the future of work.

As technology accelerates, the global workforce is facing a growing instability problem: skills that once defined entire careers are becoming obsolete in shorter cycles than education and training systems can adapt. This disconnect is creating inefficiency in education, hiring, and economic planning at a global scale.

AdaRSS addresses this by introducing a new layer of intelligence for skills.

We classify every skill based on its long-term economic durability:

* Skills that remain foundational across decades
* Skills that are rapidly growing in importance
* Skills that are likely to become obsolete

This allows individuals, institutions, and governments to make informed decisions about learning, hiring, and workforce investment.

Beyond classification, AdaRSS provides personalized career intelligence. By analyzing a user’s background and comparing it with evolving skill landscapes, we help determine whether to deepen expertise, pivot strategically, or reskill entirely.

We are building AdaRSS as infrastructure—not a standalone application. It is designed to integrate into education systems, workforce platforms, and enterprise tools through a scalable API layer.

Our mission is simple:

To reduce global workforce instability by making skill durability measurable, predictable, and actionable.

AdaRSS is currently in active development with a working production system that includes machine learning models, API infrastructure, and enterprise dashboards. We are now expanding into large-scale data training and institutional partnerships to bring this system into real-world deployment.
